2. Eligibility

To participate, you must be 18 years or older, a U.S. resident, and not reside in states that restrict paid fantasy contests (including but not limited to: Washington, Idaho, Nevada). Only one account per person is permitted. We may request proof of eligibility.

3. Game of Skill

Pick 4 Baseball is a game of skill. Outcomes depend on player knowledge, statistical understanding, and strategy in selecting weekly picks.

4. Entry Fees & Prize Pools

Players pay either $5 or $10 per week to participate. Entry fees are allocated as follows:

  • 80% → Weekly Prize Pool
  • 10% → End‑of‑Year Prize Pool
  • 10% → Game maintenance, hosting, development, and administrative costs

We do not participate in the prize pool. All allocations are transparent and fixed.

5. Payouts & Rollover Rules

If there is at least one weekly winner, the Weekly Prize Pool is paid out according to the published rules. If there is no weekly winner:

  • 75% of that week’s prize pool rolls over to the next week
  • 25% is added to the End‑of‑Year Prize Pool

This rollover continues until a weekly winner is determined. We may correct scoring errors or adjust results if MLB statistics are updated or corrected.

If the Pick4Baseball game itself (not MLB games) is suspended, paused, or permanently discontinued, refunds may be issued at our discretion for unplayed weeks.
