About PICK 4 Baseball

How It Started

PICK 4 Baseball began the same way a lot of great ideas do — with friends, spreadsheets, and a whole lot of chaos. What started as a weekly MLB prediction game tracked manually in Excel and settled through Venmo quickly grew into something bigger.

We built PICK 4 to take that same friendly competition and give it structure: automated scoring, clean leaderboards, secure payments, and a polished experience that keeps the fun while removing the hassle.

It’s still the same game at heart — pick your players, compete with friends, and try to win their money — but now it’s fast, fair, and built for the entire season.
