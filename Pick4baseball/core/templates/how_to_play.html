HOW TO PLAY

Everything you need to know about Baseball Pick 4

Game Overview

What is Baseball Pick 4?

Baseball Pick 4 is a team-based fantasy baseball game where you compete with friends to predict which players will achieve specific stats during Saturday games.

Key Features:

  • Play on teams with friends, family, or coworkers
  • Make 4 picks every week for Saturday's MLB games
  • Win weekly prizes for perfect picks
  • Compete for the season championship
  • Team captains set the weekly entry fee ($5 or $10)

How to Play

1. Join or Create a Team

Ask your team captain for the join code, or create your own team if you're the captain. Teams need a minimum of 3 players.

2. Pay Your Weekly Entry Fee

Each week, pay your team's entry fee ($5 or $10) to play that week. No payment = no picks count toward prizes.

3. Make Your Picks

Before the deadline (Saturday 11 AM in your timezone), select ONE player for each of the 4 categories.

4. Watch Saturday Games

Cheer on your picks as MLB games happen on Saturday!

5. Check Results

After games end, check to see if your picks hit or missed. Get all 4 right = you win!

6. Collect Winnings

If you got a perfect 4/4, you'll split the weekly pool with any other perfect picks. Payouts are sent automatically (if enabled) or by your team captain.

If nobody gets 4/4 in a week, the weekly pool rolls over to the next week! This means the next week's prize gets bigger and bigger until someone wins.

If the final week (Week 26) has no winner, the rollover gets added to the season championship pot!

Frequently Asked Questions

What if a player doesn't play on Saturday?

If your player doesn't play (benched, injured, etc.), your pick automatically misses for that category. Choose carefully!

Can I pick the same player twice?

Yes! If it's a two-way player like Shohei Ohtani, you can pick him for both batting (2H/HR) and pitching (SWP) categories.

What happens if I forget to pay?

Your picks will still be recorded, but they won't count toward prizes that week. You'll still accumulate points for season standings though!

How do I know if I won?

Check the app after Saturday games are complete. Results are updated and winners are notified. Your team captain will send payouts if you won!

Can I change my picks after submitting?

No. All picks are final once submitted. Double-check before you submit!
